ABOUT US

The IoT Applications Club at Jadavpur University (IOTA JU), founded in 2023, is driven by a compelling mission: to unite enthusiastic engineering students dedicated to pioneering research and development within the dynamic realm of the Internet of Things (IoT). IOTA JU is devoted to cultivating an inspiring environment that fosters not only the technical skills but also the core values essential for the academic and professional advancement of students in diverse IoT disciplines. Our expansive focus encompasses exciting fields such as Robotics, Sensors, Communication Systems, and Networking, while also delving into crucial software development domains, including Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning (AI/ML), Cloud Computing, and Data Science. At this club, students embark on a transformative journey, where they are equipped with the knowledge and experience necessary to thrive and innovate in the rapidly changing landscape of technology.
