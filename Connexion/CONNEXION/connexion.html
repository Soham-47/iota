Connexion

Connexion is the annual flagship hackathon organized by the IoT Applications Club, Jadavpur University, bringing together budding innovators to explore the limitless potential of the Internet of Things. Designed to foster creativity, collaboration, and technical excellence, Connexion invites students to solve real-world problems through smart, impactful IoT-based solutions. From hardware prototyping and cloud integration to sensor networks and intelligent automation, participants dive deep into every layer of IoT. With mentorship from industry experts, engaging workshops, and a platform to showcase talent, Connexion serves as a launchpad for future tech leaders.
