Lord of The Ring

As part of Jadavpur University’s annual techfest Srijan in March 2025, IOTA JU presented the adrenaline-charged Robo War competition, “Lord of the Ring.” This thrilling event saw teams of five battling it out in either wired or wireless formats, engineering and controlling their custom-built robots in a dynamic circular arena. With over 130 fierce contenders, the competition followed an intense knockout format, featuring three gripping rounds per match. The arena roared with energy as robots clashed in high-stakes duels, brilliantly showcasing participants’ innovation, design prowess, and technical control.
